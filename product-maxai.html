Tap into Over a Decade of Intelligence

MaxAi leverages 10+ years of patient behavior data to recommend the right actions at the right time—so you can effortlessly fill gaps in your schedule.

Start Every Day with a Plan

Before your team even walks into the practice, MaxAi generates a personalized action plan to help them hit the ground running—and keep your schedule on track.

See 30 Days Ahead—And Stay Ahead

MaxAi looks up to 30 days into the future, identifying potential slowdowns before they happen, so you can keep your schedule full with confidence.

Smart Texting, Simplified

No need to draft replies on the fly—MaxAi suggests AI-powered text prompts based on each conversation. Just choose the best response and hit send.

Focus on the Right Patients, First

MaxAi prioritizes patient outreach by analyzing who’s most likely to book—so your team can focus their energy where it counts.

Always On.
Always Scanning.

MaxAi continuously monitors your entire schedule, flagging open slots for hygiene and restorative appointments in real time.

Faster Cancellation Recovery

MaxAi Cancellation Recovery quickly identifies the right patients to fill last-minute gaps, factoring in department capacity, recent contact history, and cancellation timelines.
